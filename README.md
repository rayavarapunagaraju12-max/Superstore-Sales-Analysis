# Superstore-Sales-Analysis
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Superstore dataset with business insights and visualizations


##  Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Superstore dataset to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and business trends.

The goal is to extract meaningful insights that help in making better business decisions.

---

##  Dataset Details

The dataset contains:

* Order and shipping details
* Customer information
* Product categories and sub-categories
* Sales data
* Region and location details

---

##  Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib

---

##  Data Analysis Process

###  Data Cleaning

* Converted date columns into datetime format
* Handled missing values
* Created new columns (Year, Month, Delivery Days)

---

###  Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

#### 1. Monthly Sales Trend

* Sales are highest in November and December
* Sales are lowest in January and February

 **Insight:** Sales show strong seasonal patterns.

---

#### 2. Category Analysis

* Technology has the highest sales
* Furniture and Office Supplies follow

 **Insight:** Technology products are the main revenue drivers.

---

#### 3. Sub-Category Analysis

* Phones and Chairs are top-selling products

 **Insight:** A few key products contribute most of the revenue.

---

#### 4. Customer Analysis

* Top customers generate significant revenue

 **Insight:** Customer retention is very important.

---

#### 5. Region Analysis

* West and East regions perform best
* South region has the lowest sales

 **Insight:** Some regions need improvement and growth strategies.

---

#### 6. Delivery Analysis

* Delivery time was calculated for each order

 **Insight:** Delivery performance can affect customer satisfaction.

---

##  Visualizations

* Line chart → Monthly sales trend
* Bar charts → Category and region analysis
* Pie chart → Segment analysis
* Scatter plot → Delivery vs sales

---

##  Key Business Insights

* Sales are seasonal (peak in Q4)
* Technology is the top-performing category
* Few customers contribute a large share of revenue
* Some regions underperform

---

##  Conclusion

This analysis helps identify important sales patterns and business opportunities, enabling better decision-making.
